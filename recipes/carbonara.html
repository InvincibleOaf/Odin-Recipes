Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Home

Description

Spaghetti carbonara is a staple of Italian cuisine. The British and Americans don't often get it right, but this version comes close.

Ingredients

Serves 3-4

  • 2 eggs
  • 100g Pecorino/Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 250g Guanciale/Pancetta, chopped into small 1cm cubes
  • 400g Spaghetti
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely crushed
  • oil
  • salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Brown the guanciale in a large, heavy-bottomed saucepan
  2. Add garlic and soften
  3. Cook spaghetti in salted water, reserving some of the cooking water
  4. Add spaghetti, eggs and cheese to the guanciale with the heat off, stirring vigorously
  5. Season with salt and black pepper
  6. Enjoy!
